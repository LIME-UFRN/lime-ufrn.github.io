This page lists the main laboratory equipment currently available at LIME (Instrumentation and Microelectronics Laboratory), organized by function.

## Laboratory Infrastructure

- **Design kits (PDKs):** STMicroelectronics, IBM, X-Fab.
- **Reference measurement instruments:** pyranometer, anemometers.
- **Development kits:** FPGA kits and assorted microcontroller platforms.
- **Assorted electronic components and sensors.**

## Measurement & Test Equipment

- **Bench power supplies:**
  - [Minipa MPL-3303M](http://www.minipa.com.br/instrumentos-de-bancada/fontes-de-alimentacao/137-mpl-3303m) - 2 units. Dual 0-32 V / 0-3 A + fixed 5 V / 3 A.
  - [Minipa MPL-3305M](http://www.minipa.com.br/instrumentos-de-bancada/fontes-de-alimentacao/139-mpl-3305m) - Dual 0-32 V / 0-3 A + fixed 5 V / 3 A.
- **Oscilloscope:**
  - [Keysight MSOX3024A](https://www.keysight.com/en/pdx-x201844-pn-MSOX3024A/mixed-signal-oscilloscope-200-mhz-4-analog-plus-16-digital-channels) - Mixed-signal oscilloscope, 200 MHz, 4 analog + 16 digital channels.
- **Digital multimeters and data acquisition:**
  - [Keysight/Agilent 34411A](https://www.keysight.com/en/pd-692679-pn-34411A/digital-multimeter-6-digit-enhanced-performance) - 6.5-digit digital multimeter.
  - [Keysight 34470A](https://www.keysight.com/en/pdx-2891643-pn-34470A/digital-multimeter-7-digit-truevolt-dmm) - 7.5-digit Truevolt digital multimeter.
  - [Agilent 34970A](https://www.keysight.com/pt/pd-1000001313%3Aepsg%3Apro-pn-34970A/data-acquisition-data-logger-switch-unit) - Data acquisition / data logger / switch unit.
  - [NI USB-6361](http://www.ni.com/en-us/support/model.usb-6361.html) - 16 AI, 2 MS/s, 2 AO, 24 DIO, 4 counters/timers.
- **Data acquisition boards (PCIe):**
  - [Advantech PCIE-1840](https://www.advantech.com/products/1-2mlk8i/pcie-1840/mod_a4a811bb-bc81-422f-a76e-15932ed6bc96) - 4-channel, 16-bit, 125 MS/s digitizer.
  - [NI PCIe-6351](https://www.ni.com/en-us/support/model.pcie-6351.html) - 16 AI (16-bit, 1.25 MS/s), 2 AO (2.86 MS/s), 24 DIO multifunction I/O.
- **Source and measurement units (SMU):**
  - [Keysight B1500A](https://www.keysight.com/en/pd-582565-pn-B1500A/semiconductor-device-analyzer) - Semiconductor device parameter analyzer mainframe.
  - [Keysight B1511B](https://www.keysight.com/en/pd-2253433-pn-B1500A-A10/source-measure-units-smus?cc=US&lc=eng) - 3 units, medium-power SMU (MPSMU).
  - [Keysight B2902A](https://www.keysight.com/en/pd-1983585-pn-B2902A/precision-source-measure-unit-2-ch-100-fa-210-v-3-a-dc-105-a-pulse) - Precision SMU, 2 channels, 100 fA, 210 V, 3 A DC / 10.5 A pulse.
- **Waveform and function generators:**
  - [Keysight 33622A](https://www.keysight.com/us/en/products/waveform-and-function-generators/trueform-series-waveform-and-function-generators.html) - Trueform, 120 MHz, 2 channels.
  - [Agilent 33521A](https://www.keysight.com/en/pd-1871159-pn-33521A/function-arbitrary-waveform-generator-30-mhz) - Function/arbitrary waveform generator, 30 MHz, 1 channel, 250 MSa/s.
- **Temperature instrumentation:**
  - [Fluke Calibration 1502A](https://www.fluke.com/en/product/calibration-tools/temperature-calibrators/fluke-calibration-1502a-1504) - Precision thermometer readout.
