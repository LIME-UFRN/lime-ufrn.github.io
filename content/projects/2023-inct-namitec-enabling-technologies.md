### INCT-NAMITEC: Nano and Microelectronics for Enabling Technologies

Large collaborative research network in nano- and microelectronics aimed at research, development, innovation, human resource training, and science outreach in enabling technologies. The project addresses micro/nanofabrication, semiconductor devices, interface circuits, and IoT-oriented applications.

#### Overview

INCT-NAMITEC is a broad collaboration network built and strengthened through previous INCT initiatives and later recognized with the INCT excellence label in a subsequent call. The current project focuses on materials, processes, new semiconductor devices, interface circuits, and IoT applications.

The scope includes applications in environmental control, soil, water and air quality monitoring, gas detection, biomedicine, and agriculture/livestock. Research topics include devices and sensors such as FinFET, BioFET, ISFET, graphene FETs, qubits, integrated photonics, silicon carbide devices, and advanced tandem solar cells.

The project also includes strong industrial interaction and international collaboration with advanced research centers and companies, supporting technological innovation and industrial competitiveness.
