### Auto Parts 4.0 (R&D&I): IoT and AI Integration for an Auto Parts Marketplace

Collaborative R&D project aimed at transforming the auto parts sector through the integration of Internet of Things, Artificial Intelligence, and data analytics. The project develops an intelligent platform to optimize the operation and management of parts and suppliers.

#### Overview

This project is carried out in partnership with ConecteLab and the company HUBBI to modernize the auto parts market through advanced digital technologies.

The proposed solution integrates IoT, Artificial Intelligence, and data analysis into an innovative marketplace platform, enabling more efficient operation, improved management of parts inventories, and better coordination with suppliers.

The project is aligned with an Industry 4.0 perspective for the auto parts sector, with emphasis on data-driven decision-making and scalable digital infrastructure.
