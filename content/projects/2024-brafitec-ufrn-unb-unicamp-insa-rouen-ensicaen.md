### BRAFITEC: UFRN / UnB / Unicamp / INSA Rouen / ENSICAEN

Research exchange project focused on undergraduate student mobility and collaborative training among Brazilian and French institutions under the CAPES/BRAFITEC agreement. The initiative includes UFRN programs in Computer Engineering, Electrical Engineering, and Mechatronics Engineering, together with ENSICAEN and INSA Rouen.

---

#### Overview
This project promotes undergraduate-level research exchange across the participating institutions, strengthening academic collaboration, joint supervision opportunities, and international research exposure for students.

UFRN acts as the coordinating institution in Brazil, with UnB and Unicamp also participating in the network. The project supports activities in engineering education and applied research through institutional cooperation.
